Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Version 1.0

1. Data We Collect

Konvo collects only what is necessary for behavioral compatibility matching. This includes display identifiers you provide, MBTI calibration responses, DIGIPIN approximate area coordinates (never GPS), and interaction ledger data within sandboxed simulations. We do not collect physical addresses, camera data, or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your behavioral parameters are used exclusively to compute Resonance Engine synergy scores and match compatible profiles. AI Twin sandbox conversations run in isolated containers — these logs are processed only for compatibility previews and are never sold, shared, or used for model training by third parties.

3. Encryption & Security

All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. In-transit data is protected under TLS 1.3. Authentication uses secure password hashing and rotating session tokens. Direct human-to-human conversations are end-to-end encrypted and inaccessible to administrators.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your behavioral data at any time from the Settings panel. You may request full data export or account termination by contacting system administrators. All requests are processed within 72 hours.

5. Location Privacy

DIGIPIN coordinates represent approximate hexagonal grid zones — never your home address. The discovery map only shows compatible profiles within your approximate area. You may enable Invisible Mode in Privacy Settings to opt out of map visibility entirely.

Security Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Version 1.0

⚠️ To report a security vulnerability, contact system operators directly through the secure contact channel. Do not post vulnerabilities publicly.

Infrastructure Security

  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all data at rest
  • Secure cryptographic password hashing with dynamic salt
  • Rotating JWT authentication tokens (15-minute expiry)
  • Strict CORS policies on all API endpoints
  • Rate-limiting on authentication routes (5 attempts per minute)

Responsible Disclosure

We follow a responsible disclosure policy. Security researchers who discover vulnerabilities should report them privately. We commit to acknowledging reports within 48 hours and providing remediation timelines. We do not pursue legal action against good-faith security researchers.

Sandbox Isolation

All AI Twin interactions run in ephemeral, isolated sandbox containers. Containers are destroyed after each session and have no persistent network access outside the compatibility scoring API. Sandbox environments undergo automated security scanning before every deployment.

Discover Your DIGIPIN

The DIGIPIN (Digital Directory of Post Offices in India) is a national geospatial grid system developed by India Post. To preserve your privacy, Konvo uses approximate DIGIPIN zones instead of exact physical locations.

How to get your code:

  1. Click the link below to open the official India Post DIGIPIN portal in a new tab.
  2. Search for your area or click on the map grid to copy your alphanumeric code (e.g. 8Y1A3B5C7D).
  3. Paste the code back into the registration or settings form.
