SECURITY ARCHITECTURE

Zero-Exposure Data Mandate

We secure your behavioral footprint. Calibrations, dialogues, and coordinates are shielded by industry-standard cryptography.

1. Approximate Neighborhoods (DIGIPIN)

We never ask for or track your exact location. Your profile is matched using approximate neighborhood grid zones to keep your personal address completely private.

2. Verified Profiles

Every account is verified through a one-time code sent to your mobile device, keeping the network completely free of fake profiles, bots, and catfishers.

3. Simulated Chat Reviews

You hold the keys. Before unlocking any chat, you can review the simulated conversation history to understand exactly why your Twin matched with them.

Safety & Privacy Mandate

To protect your peace of mind, Konvo operates on a zero-exposure data policy. Your profile calibrations and answers are encrypted. Match simulations run in secure, private spaces that only share vibe scores, never exposing raw answers or your private identity until you both approve.

Authentication mechanisms use industry-standard security protocols to block dictionary scanning and automated scraping, ensuring your dating experience remains authentic and protected.

Verify configuration parameters inside the public configuration file at security.txt or contact our support team.

What exactly is Konvo?
Konvo is a compatibility-first human connection platform designed for professionals, engineers, and creators who value alignment and want to avoid superficial swiping. We match users based on deep personality, values, and communication calibrations.
Does the AI Twin talk to other real people?
Never. Your AI Twin only converses with other AI Twins inside secure sandboxed environments to pre-simulate compatibility chemistry. Direct human communication is strictly human-to-human, unlocked only after dual approval.
How are exact locations protected?
We utilize geospatial masking using approximate hexagonal areas (DIGIPIN indices). The discovery map shows other compatible entities inside these approximate grids, never revealing your home address or physical location.
Is the data sold or used for model training?
No. All behavioral parameters, MBTI assessments, and chat ledgers are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. We do not sell user behavioral insights or use them for third-party model training.
