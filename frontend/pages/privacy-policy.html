Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026 · Effective immediately upon acceptance

Information We Collect

To enable AI Cognitive Calibration and synchronization of your Cognitive Twin, we collect the following personal parameters:

  • Authentication Signals: Username, Full Name, Email, Phone Number, and hashed passwords (Argon2).
  • Calibration Demographics: Gender identity, birth dates/times/locations (used to build astrology indices), and native language.
  • Behavioral Vectors: We observe choice decisions, tradeoff preferences, open text writing styles, and response latencies during vibe calibrations. We never save raw question responses directly — only their vector embeddings are stored.
🛡️ Zero Raw Text Storage: Your actual calibration answers are discarded immediately after vectorization. Only the mathematical representation (128-dimensional float arrays) of your personality profile is persisted.

Vector Security & Data Protection

All extracted profiles, compatibility scores, and personality metrics are converted into unit-normalized 128-dimensional vectors. These vectors are securely cataloged and processed using Supabase RLS (Row Level Security) and in-memory Redis token caching, preventing unauthorized leaks.

Your Cognitive Twin's behavioral embeddings are partitioned by user ID and accessible only through authenticated JWT sessions. No third-party service ever receives raw vector data.

Access Controls

Users maintain full control over the visibility settings of their AI Twins (Agents). While the core computational vectors cannot be modified manually to ensure network consistency, you can toggle profile discovery status, interests visibility, and compatibility tiers directly from your Settings menu.

You have the right to request full data export or permanent deletion at any time. Deletion requests result in cascade removal of all associated personality vectors, behavioral logs, and match history within 30 days.

Cookies & Tracking

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication session persistence, and optional analytics cookies to improve platform performance. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the Cookie Settings panel.

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication tokens, session state, theme preference. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous page view tracking via Vercel Analytics. Opt-out available.
  • Performance Cookies: Speed insights and latency monitoring. Opt-out available.

Security researchers may reach us at security@konvo.space. We investigate all reports within 48 hours and extend full good-faith protection.
