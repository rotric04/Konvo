2. The Layman Guide: How Konvo Works

Most connection apps dump your raw profile, messages, and coordinates in cleartext databases. Here at Konvo, we believe that is a recipe for social embarrassment. Instead, we convert your very existence into math. Here is the lifecycle of your data:

1. AUTHENTICATION (OTP + HASH)
Hashed with Argon2. Verified via Resend API OTP. Hacker-proof.
↓
2. COGNITIVE CALIBRATION
Gemini presents Scenarios & Tradeoffs. Measures latency & writing style.
↓
3. 128-DIMENSIONAL VECTOR SYNTHESIS
Converted to 8 domain matrices of 128 floats. Raw text answers are discarded.
↓
4. COGNITIVE TWIN ACTIVATION (SANDBOX)
Your AI Agent Twin negotiates dates inside secure, private virtual instances.
↓
5. MUTUAL HUMAN ALIGNMENT
Only when twins report compatibility > 60% do we unlock direct human chat.

3. Cryptographic Safeguards (The Funny Details)

Argon2 Password Hash: We scramble your password into a mathematical mush. Even if a supercomputer tries to crack it, the computer will get bored and melt first.

Vectorization Anonymity: Because we only store 128-dimensional floats (like [0.12, -0.45, 0.89...]) in our Supabase databases, an intruder looking at your record would just see an abstract spreadsheet. You are essentially a series of 1,024 floating points. Good luck deciphering what your Friday night mood implies from that!

Geospatial DIGIPIN Segmenting: Instead of transmitting your exact location, we map you to a broad alphanumeric segment grid using India Post's DIGIPIN. We know you are close by, but we don't know if you're in the living room or the kitchen.
