The Layman Guide: How Konvo Works

Most connection apps dump your raw profile, messages, and coordinates in cleartext databases. Here at Konvo, we believe that is a recipe for social embarrassment and potential identity theft. Instead, we convert your very existence into math. Here's the full lifecycle of your data:

1

Authentication (OTP + Hash)

Your password is hashed with Argon2. Identity verified via Resend API OTP. Session tokens stored in Redis with TTL. Hacker-proof, and also hacker-bored.

↓
2

Cognitive Calibration (Gemini AI Engine)

Gemini presents dynamic scenarios & philosophical tradeoffs. Measures response latency & writing style. It's like a personality test that doesn't feel like one.

↓
3

128-Dimensional Vector Synthesis

Your responses are converted to 8 domain matrices of 128 floats each. Raw text answers are immediately discarded. You are now beautiful mathematics.

↓
4

Cognitive Twin Activation (Sandbox)

Your AI Agent Twin enters the global resonance grid and negotiates virtual dates inside secure, isolated, private sandbox instances. No raw identity exposed.

↓
5

Mutual Human Alignment

Only when both twins report compatibility > 60% do we unlock direct human chat. No awkward intros. The math did the hard work already.

"Think of it this way: you sent a very sophisticated math version of yourself to do the dating legwork. If the numbers vibe, you show up. If they don't - your math twin takes the rejection so you don't have to. Truly the future we deserve."

Cryptographic Safeguards (The Funny Details)

Argon2 Password Hashing: We scramble your password into a mathematical mush so dense that even a GPU farm trying to crack it would submit its resignation letter first. Argon2 is memory-hard by design - hackers need RAM they don't have.

"A hacker looking at your Argon2 hash would just see: $argon2id$v=19$m=65536,t=3,p=4$.... They'd probably close the laptop and go touch grass."

Vectorization Anonymity: Because we only store 128-dimensional floats (like [0.12, -0.45, 0.89...]) in our databases, an intruder looking at your record would see an abstract spreadsheet. You are essentially a series of 1,024 floating point numbers. Good luck figuring out what your Friday night mood implies from that.

Geospatial DIGIPIN Segmenting: Instead of transmitting your exact location, we map you to a broad alphanumeric segment grid using India Post's DIGIPIN. We know you're nearby, but we don't know if you're in the living room or the kitchen - and we prefer it that way.

JWT Session Tokens: All API calls use short-lived JWT tokens rotated per session. Even if someone intercepted a token, it expires before they finish reading it.
